SheCodes 2025
National Conference on Technical Future Of Female Youth
Purpose & Introduction

SheCodes 2025 aims to create a vibrant platform for all the female candidates all over India to share groundbreaking work in technology, design, data analysis, and more. Whether you're publishing your own website or have strong skills in coding — this conference brings minds together to spark innovation, growth, and recognition.

Keynote Speaker
Dr. Vidhya Balan
Professor, Department of AI & Data Science, IIT Bombay
With over 15 years of experience and having core coding skills, Dr. Balan is a renowned voice in artificial intelligence, digital ethics, and future technologies. Her talk on “The Future Of Female in Technical Field” is not to be missed!
